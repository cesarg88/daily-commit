# Founder Dogfooding Checklist

Use this checklist during the two-week founder MVP pass.

## Daily

- Sign in without friction from the current founder device.
- Configure the day without needing external notes to understand the UI.
- Confirm the base commitment feels deliberate, not like a generic task list.
- Update Today throughout the day on mobile.
- Confirm incomplete base objectives stay visible even when bonus work lifts the score.
- Notice whether any label, button, or warning feels ambiguous enough to slow the flow down.

## Closure

- Confirm the day closes when all base objectives are complete.
- Confirm closed days look final and do not invite accidental edits from Today.
- Confirm excluded days feel explicit and understandable.

## Weekly review

- Confirm the week view feels honest about quality versus consistency.
- Confirm unconfigured days are visible but do not distort performance.
- Confirm excluded days appear separately from scored days.

## Friction log

Record any moment where you had to stop and think about:

- where to tap next;
- what a validation warning meant;
- whether a progress update succeeded;
- whether the app reflected the real-world commitment you intended.

## Not part of this pass

- new product scope;
- analytics instrumentation;
- reminders or notifications;
- streak mechanics;
- AI assistance;
- templates or integrations;
- offline-first behavior.
